The Three Best Movies

According to Hoang Tran

1. Seven Samurai

Directed by Akira Kurosawa

One of the grandfathers of action films. A truly inentive and innovative showcase of cinema

2. The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Directed by Peter Jackson

While this is technically three films, I consider them to be one entire masterpeice.

3. La Passion de Jeanne d'Arc

Directed by Carl Theodor Dreyer

A brilliant silent film enhanced by its incredible cinematography and acting.
